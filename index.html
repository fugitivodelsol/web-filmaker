About

Graduated from the Technical Degree in Audiovisuals from UTU-LATU, with solid knowledge in audiovisual production, video editing, and technical equipment management. Holds a Diploma in Documentary Cinema from the School of Cinema of Uruguay (ECU) with experience in creating visual content that effectively communicates authentic and meaningful stories. Passionate about the power of cinema as a tool for social change, committed to exploring new visual narratives that create impact. In 2015, directed the music video "La Colina" by the Uruguayan rock band Los Oxford, winning the best music video award at the FENACIES Film Festival. In 2019, directed "El Polaco," a documentary that won the ICAU audience award for best documentary at the UN MES CORTO Film Festival. In 2021, directed the music video "Llegaste al Sur" featuring Daniel Jacques and starring actor Cesar Troncoso, which participated in various film festivals. Currently, they are producing their first feature film, PISO16, and shooting their second documentary, Las Ausencias.
