About

Graduated from the Higher Institute Polo Educativo Tecnológico UTU-LATU as a Technician in Audiovisual Production, with strong expertise in video production, editing, and technical team management. Also holding a Diploma in Documentary Filmmaking from the Escuela de Cine del Uruguay (ECU), Juan Peirano has extensive experience in creating visual content that effectively communicates authentic and meaningful stories. Passionate about the power of cinema as a tool that challenges perceptions of reality, is dedicated to exploring new visual narratives that create impact. Currently, they are producing their first feature film PISO16 and filming their second documentary Las Ausencias.
