About

Juan Peirano, Audiovisual Producer graduated from the Audiovisual Technical Program at UTU-LATU, has directed several music videos for Uruguayan artists. In 2019, he directed his first documentary "El Polaco," which won the ICAU award for best national short film as voted by the public. In 2021, he directed the music video "Llegaste al Sur" by Daniel Jacques, starring actor Cesar Troncoso. In 2023, he directed "Noche serena," a fiction-suspense short film, premiered at the DETOUR new cinema festival. Currently, he is enrolled in the Documentary Film Diploma at the Uruguay Film School. He is working on pre-production for his first feature film "PISO16" and filming his second documentary "Las Ausencias."
